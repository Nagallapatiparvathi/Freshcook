Chicken Caesar Salad

A classic Caesar salad with grilled chicken, crisp romaine, and a tangy Caesar dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup grilled chicken, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Croutons
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the romaine lettuce, grilled chicken, and Caesar dressing.
  2. Toss to coat evenly.
  3. Top with Parmesan cheese and croutons.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately.

Chicken Tikka Masala

A rich and creamy Indian dish featuring marinated chicken in a spiced tomato cream sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala spice mix
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and tikka masala spice mix for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the chicken until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until softened.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the cream and return the chicken to the pan. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice or naan.

Vegetarian Chili

A hearty and spicy chili packed with beans, vegetables, and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cans (400g each) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (400g) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add bell pepper and cook for a few more minutes.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, beans, corn, chili powder, and cumin.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with cilantro before serving.

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Bell peppers stuffed with a savory mixture of rice, vegetables, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large bell peppers
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup cooked ground beef or turkey
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook onion and garlic until softened.
  4. Add ground beef or turkey, diced tomatoes, and oregano. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in cooked rice and cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Stuff the bell peppers with the mixture and place in a baking dish.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until peppers are tender.

Veggie Stir-Fry

A quick and colorful stir-fry with a variety of fresh vegetables and a savory sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add bell pepper, broccoli, snap peas, and carrot. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Mix in soy sauce and hoisin sauce.
  5. Stir in the cornstarch mixture to thicken the sauce.
  6. Serve over cooked rice.
